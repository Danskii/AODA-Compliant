Part 6: Building Heading Structure

  1. Click on the tags option in the left hand navigation pane
  2. Right click on the tags tree and select your heading level and title and click OK
  3. Build the heading level according to sections
  4. Heading structures must proceed in numerical order from H1, H2, H3 and so on, putting the headings out of order will cause the heading check to fail
