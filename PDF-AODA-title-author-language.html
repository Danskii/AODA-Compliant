Part 2: Title, Author and Language

  1. Open the PDF you wish to remediate
  2. Navigate to File on the top toolbar → Properties
  1. Fill in the Title and Author and Keywords
  1. Click on the Advanced tab
  2. Select the correct language for your document
  3. Click OK to save
